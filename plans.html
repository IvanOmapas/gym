chest

Flat Bench Press

Lie on a bench and push weights away from your chest, targeting chest, shoulders, and triceps. Like pushing something heavy while lying down.

Incline Bench Press

Similar to flat bench press, but on an inclined bench. Targets upper chest more. Like pushing something heavy on a slightly angled surface.

Dumbell Fly

Lie on a bench, hold dumbbells, extend arms to the sides, then bring weights together in front of your chest, targeting chest muscles.

Dumbell Pullover

Lie on a bench, hold a dumbbell overhead, and lower it behind your head, targeting the chest. Like stretching a weight behind your head.

Incline Dumbell Press

Sit on an inclined bench, hold dumbbells, and push them up above your chest, targeting the upper chest muscles. Like pushing weights while sitting at an angle.

Mid Cable Crossover

Stand between cable machines, hold handles, and pull them across your chest, targeting the chest muscles. Like hugging a tree while standing at a forward angle.

Pushups

Start in a plank position, lower your body by bending your arms, and push back up, targeting the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Like pushing your body up from the ground.

Chest Dips

Hold onto parallel bars, lower your body by bending your arms, and push back up, targeting the chest and triceps. Like dipping your body between two bars.

SHOULDER

Bent Over Lteral Raise

Bend forward at the waist, hold weights with arms extended downward, and lift them out to the sides, targeting the rear shoulder muscles. Like lifting weights out to the sides.

front dumbell raise

Stand with weights in front of your thighs, raise them straight in front of you to shoulder level, targeting the front shoulder muscles. Like lifting weights in front of you.

lateral raise

Stand with weights by your sides, raise them out to the sides until parallel to the ground, targeting the side shoulder muscles. Like lifting weights out to the sides.

shoulder press

Stand or sit, hold weights at shoulder level, and push them overhead, targeting the shoulder muscles. Like lifting weights from shoulder to above your head.

arms

bicep curls

Hold weights with palms facing upward, bend your arms at the elbows, and lift the weights toward your shoulders, targeting the bicep muscles. Like curling weights in an upward motion to your shoulders.

hammer curls

Hold weights with palms facing inward, bend your arms at the elbows, and lift the weights toward your shoulders, targeting the bicep muscles. Like curling weights up toward your shoulders with a neutral grip.

tricep pushdown

Stand facing a cable machine with a rope or bar attached. Grab the rope or bar with palms facing downward, and push it down until your arms are fully extended, targeting the tricep muscles. Like pushing the rope or bar down while standing.

tricep dips

Sit on a bench or chair, place your hands on the edge, and extend your legs out in front of you. Lower your body by bending your elbows, then push back up, targeting the tricep muscles. Like dipping your body down and up using your arms.

back

deadlift

Stand with a barbell on the ground, bend your knees and hips, grip the bar, and lift it while keeping your back straight, targeting the muscles of the back and lower body. Like lifting a heavy object from the ground.

dumbbell bent over rows

Bend your knees slightly, lean forward at the waist, hold weights with palms facing inward, and pull them toward your torso, targeting the back muscles. Like rowing a boat while keeping your back straight.

lat pulldowns

Sit at a lat pulldown machine, grip the bar with your hands wider than shoulder-width apart, and pull the bar down toward your chest, targeting the muscles of the upper back. Like pulling a bar down toward your chest while seated.

pullups

Hang from a bar with your palms facing away, then pull your body upward until your chin reaches or clears the bar, targeting the back and arm muscles. Like pulling your body in an upward motion towards a bar.

legs

barbell squat

Load a barbell with weights, position it across your upper back, stand with feet shoulder-width apart, lower your body by bending your knees and hips, then push back up. This exercise targets the muscles of the legs and glutes, similar to sitting back and standing up from a weighted barbell.

Barbell Split Squat

Stand with one foot positioned forward and the other foot positioned back on an elevated surface, hold a barbell across your upper back, lower your body by bending your front knee, then push back up. This exercise targets the muscles of the legs and glutes, similar to performing a lunge.

leg extension

Sit on a leg extension machine, adjust the pad against your lower legs, and extend your legs into an upward angle against the resistance of the weight. This exercise primarily targets the quadriceps muscles, simulating the action of extending your knees against resistance.

leg press

Sit on a leg press machine, place your feet on the footplate, and push the footplate away from your body by extending your legs. This exercise primarily targets the muscles of the legs, similar to pushing a weighted platform away with your legs while seated.
